Registration

Registration fee is $165 ($99 for students). Both prices include GST and cover: a small conference dinner on Sunday night, tea/coffee and lunch on Monday and proceedings. (Details to be announced.)

Full registrations may be paid online with a credit card or via fax.

To claim student discount, please choose the fax payment option and include (STUDENT) after your organisation name. (The registration interface doesn't know about student discounts so it will insist on the fee being $165.00.) Print the tax invoice form, clearly mark it with "STUDENT REGISTRATION", and alter the amount to $99.00, then fax to the address shown.

Click here to register (for both full and student registrations).

Location

ADCS2003 will be held at the CSIRO Discovery Centre off Clunies Ross St in Acton, ACT, opposite the ANU campus. Times and program will be advised ASAP (Apologies for delay). Conference dinner will be Sunday night. Venue to be arranged.

Accommodation

Details to be finalised but we're heading for two options: (A) Bruce Hall student residence on the ANU campus opposite the Discovery Centre. (B) Rydges Lakeside Hotel.
